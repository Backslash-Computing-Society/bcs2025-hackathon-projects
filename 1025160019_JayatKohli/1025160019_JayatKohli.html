About Bora Bora

Bora Bora is a small South Pacific island northwest of Tahiti in French Polynesia. Surrounded by sand-fringed motus (islets) and a turquoise lagoon protected by a coral reef, it's known for its scuba diving and luxury resorts.

Often called the "Pearl of the Pacific," Bora Bora offers an intoxicating blend of pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, and lush tropical landscapes. The island's iconic Mount Otemanu rises dramatically from the center, creating a breathtaking backdrop for unforgettable memories.

Whether you're seeking romance, adventure, or pure relaxation, Bora Bora delivers an unparalleled tropical experience that will leave you planning your return before you've even left.

Things to Do in Bora Bora

Dive Into Adventure

🤿 Snorkeling & Diving

Explore vibrant coral gardens and swim alongside tropical fish, rays, and sharks in the crystal-clear lagoon. The underwater world of Bora Bora is absolutely magical.

Luxury Awaits

🏖️ Overwater Bungalows

Stay in iconic overwater bungalows with glass floors, private decks, and direct lagoon access. Wake up to stunning views and fall asleep to gentle waves.

Conquer the Peak

🥾 Mount Otemanu Hiking

Trek through lush tropical forests to reach breathtaking viewpoints. The challenging hike rewards you with panoramic views of the island and lagoon.

Feel the Thrill

🏄 Water Sports

Jet skiing, paddleboarding, kayaking, and parasailing. The calm lagoon provides perfect conditions for all kinds of water adventures and fun.

Romantic Bliss

🌅 Sunset Cruises

Sail around the island during golden hour, enjoying champagne and breathtaking views. Perfect for couples seeking romance and unforgettable moments.

Embrace Culture

🌺 Polynesian Culture

Experience traditional dance performances, local cuisine, and artisan crafts. Visit Vaitape village to immerse yourself in authentic island culture.
