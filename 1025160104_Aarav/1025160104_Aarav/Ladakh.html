About The Place

Ladakh is a mountainous region in North India, known for its stunning landscapes, ancient monasteries, and strong Tibetan cultural influence, earning it the nickname "Little Tibet". The region's capital is Leh and it is famous for breathtaking clear skies, high mountain passes, adventure activities, and vibrant Buddhist festivals.
